SERVING SOUTH AFRICAN  TRADITIONAL FOOD WITH A MODERN TWIST
1.ORGANISATION OVERVIEW.
•	NAME: SMILEY THOUGHTS CLOUD KITCHEN.

•	BRIEF HISTORY OF THE RESTAURANT:

Smiley thought cloud kitchen was founded in the year 2015 in the heart of Nelspruit, by the wonderful chef Nozipho Simelane. Chef Nozipho was passionate about preserving south African culinary traditions and also adding some modern twist to it.

The founder started by serving warmly homemade dishes such as pap, cabbage, chicken stew as testers   to the community, therefore this resulted to the restaurant growing in popularity, and becoming a place where local & tourists can experience a taste of local traditional food with a modern twist to it.

•	MISSION AND VISION STATEMENT.
•	VISION: The business vision is to become a leading destination for authentic south African cuisine and creating a welcoming space where people will experience traditional food that have new flavors.
•	MISSION: The business mission is to serve delicious traditional food made from local fresh ingredients, while creating a warm and friendly environment where everyone will feel welcome.
•	TARGET AUDIENCE: Our main targets are young people, students who are looking for affordable and tasty meals, local families and tourists.
2. WEBSITE GOALS AND OBJECTIVES.
  The business websites goals is to attract new customers and encourage them to visit the restaurant. Promote traditional food and showcase south African dishes and culture through photos. And lastly to provide information, share the menu and prices, location and operating hours.
3. PROPOSED WEBSITE FEATURES AND FUNCTIONALITY.
•	The website will have essential features such as:
•	HOMEPAGE: the website will have various things like the business logo, slogan and welcome page.it will show our popular dishes and current running specials lastly this website will have quick links to our menu, delivery options and our contacts.
•	ABOUT US: this part will outline the story background, mission, vision and also give an brief summary about the founder chef Nozipho and alongside her team.
•	CONTACTS: this part of the website will include our business address, phone number, email and all of our social media platforms along with their links.

•	SERVICES: this part will include our different forms of delivery and collection options, the business operating hours, the different special deals, our private catering services and our weekly cooking classes.

•	THE MAIN FUNCTIONALITY: the desired purpose of this website is for it to be a responsive design. meaning it will be able to function optimal in all different kind of device (cellphones, tablets, and desktops.it will also have a friendly user navigation bar that consist of all essential features. 4. DESIGN AND USER EXPERIENCE
•	Color scheme: the main background color for the website is soft peach mixed with beige, which gives the website an warm and welcoming feeling.
•	Typography: the fonts used vary from capital letters to small letters. the black texts on the beige background creates a very good contrast. Because of the headings being bigger and bold while the menu items are in small letters the customer attention will be grasped.
•	Layout and design: the header WELCOME TO SMILEY THOUGHTS CLOUD KITCHEN, is large and clear at the top. the header is then followed by an food image and our slogan
•	User experience: the website will be easy to read and comprehend since the fonts are simple and the contrast is good. The website is well organized because the menus are structured by days making customers to be aware of what is being served on that specific day.
